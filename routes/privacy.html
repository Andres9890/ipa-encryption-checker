1. Introduction

At IPA Encryption Checker, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website located at https://andres9890.github.io/ipa-encryption-checker/ (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we only collect information from the IPA files you voluntarily upload for analysis. We do not collect any personal information or usage data from our users.

3. Uploaded Files

Our primary function is to analyze IPA files that you upload to our Service. When you upload an IPA file:

  • The file is temporarily stored on cloudflare servers for analysis purposes only
  • We extract metadata from the file (such as app name, bundle identifier, version, etc.)
  • We analyze the file's encryption status
  • We generate an MD5 hash of the file
  • All files are automatically deleted after analysis is complete or within 24 hours, whichever comes first

4. Usage Data

We do not collect any usage data or technical information when you visit our website. We do not track:

  • IP addresses

  • Pages visited

Our focus is solely on providing the IPA analysis service without collecting any information about your browsing behavior or identity.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

7. Data Retention

We retain your uploaded files only for the time necessary to perform the requested analysis, and in no case longer than 24 hours. After this period, all uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers.

Since we do not collect any usage data, there is no additional information retained beyond the temporary storage of your uploaded IPA files.
